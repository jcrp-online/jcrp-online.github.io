Server Rules and Guidelines

Welcome to our server! To ensure a positive and respectful environment for everyone, please adhere to the following rules:


In-Game Conduct

  • Maintain Character: All players are required to remain in character at all times while in the city.
  • Respect Others: Treat all players with respect, regardless of their tenure in the city. Experienced players are encouraged to set a positive example for newcomers.
  • No Abusive Language: The use of strong, offensive, or discriminatory language (including racist remarks) is strictly prohibited. We have zero tolerance for racism or any form of discrimination.

Discord and Staff Interaction

  • Discord Rules Apply: All rules regarding respectful conduct and honesty extend to our Discord server.
  • Administrator and Staff Respect: Do not abuse, annoy, or excessively message administrators or staff members. They are often busy assisting other players and managing the server. Only contact them if you genuinely need their assistance.
  • New Player Assistance: Administrators, staff, and experienced players are expected to provide new players with a full city tour and guidance.

Disciplinary Actions

  • Warnings: Violations of these rules will result in a warning.
  • Temporary Ban: Accumulating three warnings will lead to a 24-hour temporary ban from the server.
  • Permanent Ban: Continued rule-breaking after a temporary ban will result in a permanent ban.

We are committed to fostering a respectful and inclusive community for all players.
