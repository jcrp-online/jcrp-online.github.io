About us:

Jupiter County RP (JCRP) is an established 18+ FiveM roleplay server and community. We offer diverse roleplay opportunities, including organized crime, motorcycle clubs, and law enforcement. Our server promotes a relaxed and inclusive environment, empowering members to craft their characters' narratives and engage in roleplay freely.


We maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy against hate speech and disrespect towards any individual, whether a staff member, a new participant, or a member of our Discord community. All members, including administrative staff, operate under the same permissions and are treated equally from the day they achieve membership, regardless of their tenure. We emphasize that this community is not the appropriate venue for those seeking rank, status, or power.


As a community, we are dedicated to fostering an environment where all members feel at home. We believe in demonstrating passion, love, and respect to uphold a civil and collaborative atmosphere. Our collective efforts are focused on continuous growth and improvement, working together to strengthen our community.

Community:

As a community, we actively seek and consider member requests for new ideas and features to be implemented in the near future. Our aim is to introduce exciting and innovative elements that enhance the realistic immersion for both new players joining the server and long-standing members exploring updated content.

Law Enforcement:

Our law enforcement department comprises diverse agencies, including City Police, Sheriff's Departments, and State Troopers, all dedicated to protecting and serving the citizens of Los Angeles and San Diego Counties.

Mafia:

A new and dangerous force has emerged on our server: The LA Mafia, claiming the streets of Los Angeles. Cross them at your own risk – they're a group you definitely don't want to mess with! WATCH OUT!!!

MC Groups:

Our server will host a diverse range of motorcycle clubs. Currently, our main club is The Mayans Northern Cali MC, under the leadership of CivKyle, and we anticipate more clubs joining the community in the near future.

Based in LA:

JCRP is set in the Los Angeles, California area. We rebranded to an LA-style server on February 20th, 2022, and we operate under California's laws and regulations.



Be a creator:

We welcome all streamers and YouTubers at JCRP! There are zero restrictions on sharing your experiences on our server, whether it's your first time or you're a long-time member. We encourage you to tell your story, grow your fanbase, and develop into the creator you want to be. There's no need to "apply for media tags" here; just jump in and create!

Departments:

Upon becoming a member at JCRP, you will begin as a civilian with the immediate ability to roleplay as Fire/EMS personnel or a dispatcher. Opportunities to join Law Enforcement (LEO) become available once specific requirements and training protocols have been successfully met.
