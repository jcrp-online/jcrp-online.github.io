About us:

Jupiter County RP (JCRP) is an established 18+ FiveM roleplay server and community. We offer diverse roleplay opportunities, including organized crime, motorcycle clubs, and law enforcement. Our server promotes a relaxed and inclusive environment, empowering members to craft their characters' narratives and engage in roleplay freely.


We maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy against hate speech and disrespect towards any individual, whether a staff member, a new participant, or a member of our Discord community. All members, including administrative staff, operate under the same permissions and are treated equally from the day they achieve membership, regardless of their tenure. We emphasize that this community is not the appropriate venue for those seeking rank, status, or power.


As a community, we are dedicated to fostering an environment where all members feel at home. We believe in demonstrating passion, love, and respect to uphold a civil and collaborative atmosphere. Our collective efforts are focused on continuous growth and improvement, working together to strengthen our community.
